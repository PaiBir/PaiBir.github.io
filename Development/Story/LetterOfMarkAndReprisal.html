Official Letter of Marque and Reprisal for the Agathodaemon

Home

We, of the Tribunal of Human Worlds, hereby grant License and Authority to Agriwulf, Captain of the Human Vessel Agathodaemon, the capacity to carry missiles and heavy firearms not granted to civilian vessels, and conduct herself in a warlike manner. As an extension of this, the Agathodaemon is hereby granted free reign to, with force, seize or destroy space-going enemies of and threats to the Tribunal of Human worlds, without oversight. The vessel is hereby able to claim an enemy's weaponry, outfitting, and resources, and to take the crew and vessel to the nearest convenient allied space station for trial and process. If the capture is judged lawful, the crew of Agathodaemon shall be granted a fitting prize, in keeping with their lawful treatment of the captured crew as according to accepted international law. Until said otherwise, this letter of Marque and Reprisal shall be considered valid.
